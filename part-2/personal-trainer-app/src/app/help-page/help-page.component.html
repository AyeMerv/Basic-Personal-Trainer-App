Privacy and Security Requirements

Your privacy and the security of your personal information are our highest priority. We are committed to protecting the personal data you share with us while using our mobile application. This Privacy and Security page outlines how we collect, store, and manage your data in compliance with Australian privacy laws and regulations, including the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

1. Collection of Personal Information

We collect personal information to provide you with a better and more personalized experience. The types of data we may collect include:

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII): Your name, email address, phone number, and emergency contact details.
  • Health Data: Information regarding your fitness goals, exercise routines, special health conditions, medication lists, allergies, and other health-related data necessary to provide personalized fitness advice and programs.
  • Device Information: Details about the device you're using (e.g., mobile phone type, operating system version, device ID).
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our app, including clicks, interactions, and app performance metrics.

We collect this information with your consent, either through direct user input or automatic collection as you interact with our app.

2. Use of Personal Information

We use the personal information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the functionality of our app, including fitness tracking, exercise suggestions, and health assessments.
  • To communicate with you regarding your account, including notifications, reminders, and updates about your fitness progress.
  • To offer customer support and resolve any issues or inquiries you may have.
  • To comply with legal obligations or regulations that require the collection of health data.

We will never use your data for purposes other than those explicitly stated here without your consent.

4. Sharing of Data

We respect your privacy and do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties. However, in limited circumstances, we may share your data:

  • With Service Providers: We may engage trusted third-party service providers to assist with certain functions of the app (e.g., cloud storage, email notifications). These service providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your personal information if required by law or in response to valid legal processes, such as subpoenas, court orders, or requests from law enforcement agencies.
  • With Your Consent: We may share your data with third parties if you have provided explicit consent, such as when using a feature that allows you to share your fitness data with a personal trainer or a healthcare provider.

We do not share your data with advertisers or other third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Your Rights and Control Over Your Data

You have the right to access, update, and delete your personal data at any time. We provide tools within the app to allow you to:

  • View and Update Your Information: You can update your contact details, fitness goals, and health data directly within the app.
  • Delete Your Account: You may request to delete your account and associated data at any time. This can be done through your account settings or by contacting us directly.
  • Withdraw Consent: If you no longer wish for us to collect your health data, you may withdraw your consent at any time. Please note that withdrawing consent may limit certain features of the app.

If you have any concerns about how your data is being used, or if you wish to exercise any of your rights, please contact us at support@app.com.

6. Data Retention

We will only retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy or as required by law. If you request the deletion of your data, we will comply in a timely manner, subject to any legal obligations that require us to retain certain records.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe that we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete it.

8. Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach involving your personal information, we will notify you promptly in accordance with Australian privacy laws. We will take immediate action to investigate the breach, assess its impact, and mitigate any potential harm. We are committed to transparency and will provide you with the necessary information about the breach and the steps being taken to protect your data.

9. Compliance with Australian Privacy Laws

We are fully committed to complying with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth), the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs), and any other applicable privacy laws in Australia. Our data collection, storage, and security practices adhere to these legal requirements, ensuring your personal and health data is handled responsibly and securely.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted within the app and on our website, and we will notify you if any significant changes are made.

By continuing to use the app, you acknowledge and agree to the terms of this privacy policy. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at support@app.com.
