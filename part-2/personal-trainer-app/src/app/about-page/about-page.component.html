Help & Information

Welcome to the Personal Trainer App!

This app helps you manage your clients, including adding, editing, deleting, and viewing their details. You can also track their fitness programs and health notes.

How to Use the App:

  • Client List: View all clients currently in the system. There is also the option to Edit and Delete clients on this page.
  • Add Client: On this page you can add a new client by providing all of their details.
  • Privacy & Security: On this page you can view all of our privacy and security information regarding information that we will be using from you and your clients.

If you have any further questions, please reach out to support.
